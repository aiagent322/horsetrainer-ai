🖥 Virtual Coaching

Online Horse Trainers & Virtual Coaching

Remote training has become a legitimate and effective option for horse owners without access to top trainers in their area. Video review, live sessions via video call, and remote training plans can make a real difference — if the trainer is right for your situation.

What Online Coaching Can & Can't Do
✅ Works Well Online
  • •Video review of your riding with feedback
  • •Groundwork and horsemanship guidance
  • •Custom training plans and homework
  • •Dressage and equitation coaching
  • •Pattern work critique (barrel racing, reining)
  • •Supplemental coaching between local lessons
⛔ Needs In-Person
  • •Dangerous behavior problems (rearing, bolting)
  • •First rides / colt starting
  • •Situations requiring physical intervention
  • •Beginner riders on problem horses
  • •Any situation with a danger rating of 3+
