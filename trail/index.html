Trail Horse Training

Building a safe, confident trail horse is one of the most practical and rewarding training goals. Whether you're starting from scratch or rebuilding confidence after a bad experience, systematic desensitization is the path.

The Trail Horse Foundation

A truly safe trail horse has been desensitized systematically — not just exposed to everything and hoped for the best. Exposure without control and pressure release creates a horse that tolerates things until it doesn't. Systematic desensitization creates a horse that genuinely isn't bothered by new things.

The foundation: your horse should be fully solid in groundwork, yielding, backing, and desensitization before any trail work begins. A horse that doesn't respond reliably on the ground will not respond reliably when its adrenaline is up on the trail.
